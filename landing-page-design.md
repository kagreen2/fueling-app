# Landing Page Redesign — Fuel Different

## Color Scheme
- **Primary**: Deep purple (#7C3AED / purple-600) — used for backgrounds, gradients, key UI
- **Accent**: Vibrant green (#22C55E / green-500) — lightning bolt, CTAs, highlights, checkmarks
- **Background**: Slate-900 (#0F172A) dark base
- **Text**: White for headings, slate-300/400 for body

## Brand Mark
- Green lightning bolt ⚡ — crisp, clear SVG icon
- Used in nav, hero, favicon, PWA icons

## Landing Page Sections

### 1. Nav (fixed, glass-morphism)
- Green ⚡ + "Fuel Different" wordmark
- Sign In (ghost) + Get Started (green accent button)

### 2. Hero
- Headline: "Fuel Your Performance"
- Sub: "Evidence-based nutrition coaching for athletes and fitness enthusiasts. Personalized macros, AI-powered meal tracking, and real-time coach communication — all in one app."
- CTA: "Get Started" (green) + "See How It Works" (outline)
- NO free trial language

### 3. Feature Grid (3 columns)
- 📸 AI Meal Tracking — Snap a photo, get instant macro breakdown with AI coaching feedback
- 🎯 Personalized Macros — ISSN evidence-based calculations using your body composition, sport, and goals
- 📊 InBody Integration — Upload your InBody scan for precision BMR and body composition tracking

### 4. How It Works (3 steps)
1. Create your profile — Choose Athlete or General Fitness, enter your stats
2. Get your plan — Personalized calorie and macro targets calculated from your body data
3. Track & improve — Log meals, check in daily, chat with your coach

### 5. For Athletes & Coaches (2-column split)
- Athletes: Personalized macros, daily check-ins, meal photo logging, hydration tracking, supplement safety reviews, InBody progress charts
- Coaches: Team dashboard, real-time alerts, nutrition management, direct messaging, biometric tracking, team invite codes

### 6. Key Capabilities (icon grid)
- Daily wellness check-ins
- AI-powered meal analysis
- Hydration monitoring
- Supplement safety reviews
- InBody body composition tracking
- Coach-athlete messaging
- Evidence-based macro targets
- Progress analytics & trends
